BudgetView in brief

BudgetView: your budget in the blink of an eye!

BudgetView gives you a clear picture of your budget, without requiring tedious manipulations or any accounting knowledge.

BudgetView displays your entire budget on a single screen, as a set of simple envelopes: income, groceries, leisures, loans, etc.

Each month, you set your own limits for each envelope. You can finely tune your budget, and make sure you get the most out of your income.

The cherry on the cake? BudgetView shows you the evolution of your budget over the incoming months. You can then anticipate any problem and make room for your most important projects!
