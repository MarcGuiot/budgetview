Can BudgetView be used as a Money replacement?

Microsoft Money has been discontinued on June 2009. If you are a Money user and are looking for a replacement, you can give BudgetView a try, keeping in mind however that the BudgetView approach is based on two main drivers:

    Work on your whole budget visually, instead of managing lists of individual transactions.
  • Forecast the evolution of your budget for the upcoming months, to prepare and realize your most important projects (trips, major purchases, investments, etc.)
