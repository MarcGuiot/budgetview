Visualize what's essential

With BudgetView, forget about the long listings of operations you'll find everywhere.
Visualize all you budget on a single screen, as a set of easy to monitor envelopes: income, groceries, leisures, etc.

Your savings and your projects first!

At the beginning of each month, prepare your budget to leave some room for your savings and your projects. The ideal solution: a fixed transfer to your savings accounts, to pay yourself first! BudgetView will then help you tune your spending to avoid any overdraw.

Notice the slightest overrun

During the course of the month, import your financial statements from your bank to check how you are doing compared to what you planned. At a glance, you can see any overrun and adjust your budget to avoid any overdraw and preserve your projects.

Anticipate the coming months

Observe the evolution of the position of your main accounts in the near future. You can anticipate any problem, and also prepare your projects to see their impact on your budget.

No tedious input of transactions

Import your financial statements from your bank's website. BudgetView categorizes automatically most of your transactions, leaving usually only a few operations to process manually. You budget is up to date in just a few minutes!

Secure and confidential

You personal information is not sent over the internet. You keep everything on your own computer, and you can protect your data with a password you'll be the only one to know.
