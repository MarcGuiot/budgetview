Visualize the essential

With BudgetView, forget about the long lists of operations you'll find everywhere. Instead, visualize your entire budget on a single screen in a simple set of envelopes such as: income, groceries, leisure, etc.

Your projects come first!

At the beginning of each month, arrange your budget to leave some room for savings and any desired financial goals. The ideal solution: set up a fixed transfer to your savings account so that you pay yourself first! BudgetView will then help you fine tune your spending to avoid any overdrafts.

Notice the slightest overrun

During the course of each month, import financial statements from your bank and compare your spending with your planned budget. In just one glance, you will be aware of any cost overrun. You can then readjust your budget to avoid overdraw and preserve your goals.

Anticipate the coming months

Observe the projected activity in your main accounts for the near future, to anticipate any problems. You can now accurately plan your goals since you will be able to see their impact on your budget.

No tedious input of transactions

Import your financial statements from your bank's website. BudgetView categorizes automatically most of your transactions, leaving only a few operations for manual processing. This way, your budget can be up to date in just a few minutes, at any time!

Secure and confidential

Your personal information is not sent over the internet: everything is kept on your own computer. Further protect your sensitive data with a password that only you will know.
