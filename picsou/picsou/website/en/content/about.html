About BudgetView

What? BudgetView makes personal budgeting as quick and easy as possible to help you not only get a clear picture of your finances, but to realize your most important financial goals as well.
Why? Because there is no reason why budgeting your finances should be such a pain, and because money alone should not prevent people from fulfilling the goals they really care about.
Who? BudgetView is developed by Marc Guiot and Régis Medina - two computer enthusiasts who think that managing one's budget can be quick and - why not? - even fun.
How? Thanks to your feedback, by identifying the obstacles you face in tuning and monitoring your budget, and then eliminating these obstacles one by one, patiently.
When? It all started in may 2006, with a small group of program users. BudgetView grew step by step, evolving and improving over time, until it took its current shape. The company was created in October 2010, and the product was made available to the public in November 2010.
Where? Our company is based in Paris, France, but we work on BudgetView everywhere we go.
