Can I enter transactions manually?

We advise to enter transactions manually only if you have no means to download statements for a given account. If you want to declare future transactions for an account that supports statements downloads, you can simply set a planned amount for the corresponding envelope.

To enter transactions, go to the "Categorization" view. At the bottom of the transactions list, you will see a "Enter transactions manually" button.

You can also set your account balance manually by clicking on the corresponding amount in the "Accounts" view.
