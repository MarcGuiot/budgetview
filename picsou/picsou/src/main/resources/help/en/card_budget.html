The Budget view presents the status of all the envelopes of your main accounts for the selected month.

Each block presents the active envelopes for the selected month.

You can edit each envelope by clicking on its name, and add new envelopes by using the "Actions" menu at the bottom of each block.

The arrows displayed at the right of each envelope show the evolution of the corresponding amount compared to the previous month. You can click on these arrows for further details.

BudgetView displays the daily position of your main accounts for the selected month and the month after.

The minimum position for these accounts is indicated for each month, to help you fine tune the planned amounts of your envelopes in order to avoid any overdraw.

You can read the tuning your budget page for further information on how to get the most of this view.

Note: The "Savings" block only displays envelopes representing transfers between your main accounts and savings accounts. Click on the "View all" button at the bottom of this block to see the complete list of envelopes for all your savings accounts.
