Follow the steps below to download your transactions from your bank's website:

  1. Go to your bank's website using an internet browser.
  2. Log on to your personal space.
  3. Navigate to the downloads page - depending on your bank, it can be accessed either from a general menu or from your main account page.
  4. Select the OFX (Microsoft Money) or QIF (Quicken) format. You can select all available transactions and use the default date format - BudgetView will automatically recognize the date format and ignore duplicate transactions.
  5. Start the download.
  6. Use the "Open with..." option if your browser supports it, the downloaded file will be automatically opened in BudgetView. You can also double-click on a file on your desktop or your file manager to open it directly in BudgetView.
