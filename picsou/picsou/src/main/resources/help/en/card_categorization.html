In the categorisation view, you assign your transactions to the envelopes defined for all budget areas.

The Operations block displays all the transactions to categorize.

Initially, all your transactions are displayed in this list. You can filter this list to see only the transactions corresponding to the selected months, those from the last import, or those which are not yet categorized.

To categorize transactions, you first have to select them in this list, and then choose the corresponding envelope in the right-hand block.

Once the transaction selected, you have to select the budget area where it belongs, and then the corresponding envelope.

You can create new envelopes by clicking the "Add envelope" button below the list of envelopes.

You can modify or delete envelopes by clicking on the magnifier icon located next to each one.

When you categorize a transation, you can also split it in several transactions or shift it to the previous or next month.
