In cases where you cannot download your transactions from your bank's website, you can enter your transactions manually in BudgetView. This can also be useful to manage a "cash" account.

To do this, you have to go to the Categorization view (select). You will find there an "Enter transactions manually" button, at the bottom of the left-hand block.

Before entering your own transactions, you have to create an account whose update mode is "Manual input". If you don't have one, BudgetView will propose you to create it.
