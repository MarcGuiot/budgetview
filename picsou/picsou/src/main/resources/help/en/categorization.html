In order to prepare and monitor your budget, you need to create the corresponding envelopes and assign them transactions you import from your bank's website or you enter manually in the application.

In the categorization view, BudgetView displays all the transactions you have to categorize.

Some envelopes are created by default when you first run the application. When categorizing your transactions, you can add new envelopes using the "Add envelope" button at the bottom of the screen.

Once most of your transactions are categorized, you can go to the Budget view in order to fine-tune your budget and start monitoring it.
