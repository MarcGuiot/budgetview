In the Dashboard view, BudgetView lets you create and manage your projects.

You can create projects for many kinds of events:

  • a trip,
  • a wedding,
  • home renovation activities,
  • the birth of a baby,
  • an important purchase,
  • etc.

When creating your project, you provide the list of purchases that will be associated to it. For instance, when planning a trip you will enter:

  • the booking for the accomodation,
  • the purchase of equipement (clothes, photography, etc.),
  • the outward journey,
  • the accomodation,
  • food,
  • visits,
  • local transportation,
  • the return trip,
  • etc.

BudgetView then creates automatically an envelope for this project, with the same name as the project, in the "Extras" budget area. You can thus monitor the expected/actual amounts for the envelope in the "Extras" block of the Budget view, and also assign transactions to this envelope in the "Extras" block of the Categorization view.
