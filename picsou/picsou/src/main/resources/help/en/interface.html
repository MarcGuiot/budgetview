BudgetView manages your budget month by month.

These months are displayed on the timeline at the top of the screen:

The selected month is displayed with a gray background. You can click on other months to select them, and select different months at one by dragging your mouse over them.

Under each month label, a color label indicates whether the minimum position planned for this month for your main accounts is positive or negative.

BudgetView is organized in five views:

The Dashboard view gives you an overview of your projects, as well as the evolution of the daily positions of your main and savings accounts.
The Budget view is the command center of your budget. You can monitor your income and your spendings in detail, month by month.
The Accounts view lets you manage all your bank accounts, along with the corresponding transactions.
The Categorization view is the place where you assign your transactions to the various envelopes of your budget.
The Analysis view provides you with an overview of the structure and evolution of your budget, with a set of interactive reports.
