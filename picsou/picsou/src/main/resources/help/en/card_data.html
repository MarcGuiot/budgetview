The Accounts view presents your banking accounts along with the corresponding transactions for the selected months.

The Main accounts and Savings accounts blocks display the list of your bank accounts. You can click on the name of an account to modify its definition. You can also click on its position to change it, in case the displayed amount is not in line with the current situation.

You can click anywhere on a an account row to select this account and see only its corresponding transactions in the right-hand view.

The Transactions block displays the list of transactions for the selected months.

You can copy/paste transactions by selecting them in the table, and using the usual keyboard shortcut for your system (Ctrl-C for Windows, Linux or Command-C for Mac).
