In order to get reliable estimates for your budget, you have to tune properly all the envelopes that constitute your budget.

Please follow the steps below to finalize your budget.

Categorize all your transactions

Make sure that most of the transactions you have imported are categorized.

Adjust your envelopes

Make sure that envelopes are present for all the expenses you have planned for the next few weeks and months. You also need to set the proper periodicity for envelopes that you use every few months or every year.

Here is a list of envelopes that frequently require some tuning:

  • Birthdays for all your family and friends, which repeat... every year.
  • Car maintenance, which needs usually to be done once a year.
  • Gas and electricity bills, which repeat every few months.
  • Taxes, in the case where you don't have monthly payments.
  • Some insurances, par exemple your home insurance, which may be paid once per year.
  • Christmas presents
  • Irregular income such as bonuses, etc.

Adjust you "Miscellaneous" envelope

You can use such an envelope to leave a "margin" amount for unanticipated expenses in your budget, and reduce it during the month when unanticipated expenses actually show up.

Import your savings accounts

Make sure that you have declared and imported all your savings accounts in BudgetView.
