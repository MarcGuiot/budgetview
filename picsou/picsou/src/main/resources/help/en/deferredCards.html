In the case of deferred debit cards:

  • the transactions made with this card are managed on a dedicated account
  • once a month, the position of this dedicated account is reset to zero, and a withdrawal transaction appears on your main account, representing the total of the transactions made with the card during that period.

This monthly operation on your main account is a potential duplicate of all the transactions already made on your card account. To prevent it from introducing errors in your budget, you have to categorize it in a specific way.

For this, you must first make sure that the card account is properly declared as a "Deferred debit card" account. To check this, you can click on the corresponding account in the Accounts view.

After that, in the Categorization view, when you select this monthly transaction, you need to select the budget area "Other". You should then find further instructions in the "Transfer to a deferred debit card account" section.
