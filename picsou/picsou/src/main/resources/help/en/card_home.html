Welcome to BudgetView!

If you use BudgetView for the first time, you should read first our introduction to the software, or our online tutorials.

The Dashboard gives you an overview of the evolution of your accounts, as well as you current and next projects.

The Projects block lets you see and edit your projects - holidays and trips, important purchases, etc. You can use the "Add project" button on the left to add a new project and see immediately its impact on your budget in the other graphs below.

The Main accounts and Savings accounts graphs show the evolution of the position of your accounts over time. You can thus anticipate any problems in the future, and react immediately.

However, in order to get meaninful information from these graphs, you need to have a good definition of your budget for the current and coming months. Please refer to our tuning your budget guide for more information.

You can double-click on a graph to navigate to the corresponding budget for that month.
