The Analysis view gives you an overall vision of the evolution and structure of your budget.

Using this view, you can:

  • explore the composition of your budget.
  • see the evolution of this budget over the coming months, and thus anticipate any problems and prepare your projects.
  • compare the budget of the current month and the one from the month before.

You can click on the "Show details" button at the bottom of this screen to reveal the table of details for all your envelopes. Click on the graphs or on the rows in this table to explore the details of your budget.
